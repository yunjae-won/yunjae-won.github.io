Research

I am primarily interested in preference optimization, continual learning, and large language models. My current research focuses on quantifying information gain to enhance continual learning and knowledge distillation.

Differential Information: An Information-Theoretic Perspective on Preference Optimization
Yunjae Won, Hyunji Lee, Hyeonbin Hwang, Minjoon Seo,
Preprint. Under Review.
arXiv

By interpreting preference optimization as the process of learning differential information, we characterize the structure of preference data, justify DPO's log-ratio reward, and explain the resulting behaviors of trained policies.
